Hello, I am Hector Williams—a dynamic professional seamlessly blending the realms of audio engineering and web development. My journey has been marked by a significant role as the owner of a recording studio and record label, On The Map Productions LLC. This role has served as the crucible for honing my craft in music production and audio engineering. My adeptness with tools such as Avid Pro Tools, Logic Pro X, and Melodyne has led to the creation and publication of exceptional music, including notable contributions to my own repertoire. Yet, my narrative extends beyond the boundaries of audio. My enthusiasm for web development drove me to complete META's Front-End Development program on Coursera, while concurrently securing certificates in "Responsive Web Design" and "Javascript Algorithms and Data Structures" from freeCodeCamp. My command over HTML, CSS, Javascript, and React, coupled with proficiency in tools like VS Code, Github, and Figma, has enabled me to craft a visually captivating and functionally adept static webpage for On The Map Productions LLC. An intriguing facet of my journey encompasses a role as a Delivery Driver for DoorDash/UberEats, where my adaptability and adept communication served as instrumental assets. My bilingual fluency in English further bolsters my capacity for effective collaboration. Central to my professional ethos is the dedication to multifaceted teamwork. This ethos, combined with a portfolio spanning music production, audio engineering, web development, and delivery services, positions me as an adept professional poised for the challenges of the creative landscape. I invite you to witness my proficiency in forging melodies, crafting immersive digital experiences, and embracing diverse roles—all united by the spirit of creativity and innovation. Let us collaborate to manifest unique and impactful ventures.
